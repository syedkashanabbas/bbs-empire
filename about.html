Welcome to BBS Empire.

BBS Empire is an artistic, musical, and cultural ecosystem founded by the artist Side BBS, also known as Simone De Lucia.

It brings together professionals and creatives who share a common vision: to give a voice to the new Italian and international Urban generation.

The collective merges music, lifestyle, communication, and personal growth in one direction — to build an empire of talent, freedom, and authenticity.

The four fundamental pillars are:
Music – production and release of Urban tracks, whether Hip Hop, Trap, Latin, or Reggaeton.
Clubbing – events, parties, and showcases tied to nightlife and live experiences.
Marketing – strategic and viral communication built on authenticity and impact.
Mindset – personal growth and inspiration for young emerging talents in music, entertainment, or any creative field, united by the desire to improve and make their voices heard.

“BBS Empire is not just music. It’s vision, mindset, and belonging.
It’s proof that every talent can become an empire.”

Born in Milan — the beating heart of Italian urban culture — and projected toward the international scene, BBS Empire unites a Latin soul with a European reality.

Every artist, producer, creative, or fan is part of a community that believes in discipline, passion, and the possibility of building something great starting from oneself.
